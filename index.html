Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?

A bookmark is a way to save a web page's URL so you can easily access it later. It is like a physical bookmark that you use to save your place in a book.

How can I request a new browser?

Contact the browser's developer: You can contact the browser's developer directly to request a new feature or to report a bug. The developer's contact information is usually available on the browser's website.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, there is a mobile app for Bookmark. You can download it on Play Store.

What about other Chronium browsers?

There are many other Chromium browsers available, each with its own unique features and benefits. You can choose the one that best suits your needs.
